About

I am a medical student at the Federal University of Health Sciences of Porto Alegre (UFCSPA), Brazil, with a research focus in evidence synthesis and clinical epidemiology. My work spans 28 PubMed-indexed publications across cardiology, hepatology, and neurology — with more than 200 citations — including contributions to diagnostic test accuracy modeling, network meta-analysis, and Bayesian approaches to evidence synthesis. I coordinate MetaHub, a bilingual (EN/PT) training program in systematic review methodology, and develop open-source R packages to support reproducible meta-analytic workflows.
